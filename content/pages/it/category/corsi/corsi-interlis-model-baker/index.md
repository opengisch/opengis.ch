## [INTERLIS Crash Course Webinar](<../../../2023/03/06/interlis-crashcourse-webinar/index.html> "INTERLIS Crash Course Webinar")
The goal of this crash course is to introduce “complete beginners” to INTERLIS. After the crash course, participants will know what INTERLIS is, how it is used, how to read a model, and how to find their way around it. They will also be able to model a simple example themselves.
