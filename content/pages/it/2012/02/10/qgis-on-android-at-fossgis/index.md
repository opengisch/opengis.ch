This March 20st I’ll be presenting the status of [QGIS on Android at FOSSGIS.DE](<https://www.fossgis.de/konferenz/2012/programm/events/415.de.html> "QGIS on Android at FOSSGISS").  
The conference will be held on 20-22 March 2012 at Hochschule Anhalt in Dessau-Rosslau and is the biggest German speaking conference about Open Source Geoinformatics.  
Registrations are open until March 16th [here](<https://www.fossgis.de/civicrm/event/register?id=2&reset=1> "fossgis registration")
