## Description
The goal of this crash course is to introduce “complete beginners” to INTERLIS. After the crash course, participants will know what INTERLIS is, how it is used, how to read a model, and how to find their way around it. They will also be able to model a simple example themselves.
The crash course lasts 3.5 hours (1:30 pm – 5:00 pm) via Google Meet (no Google account required) and costs 150 CHF per person. The course takes place with a minimum of 5 participants.
[Register now](</it/calendario-corsi/>)
## **Prerequisites**
None.
## **Software**
None. The webinar is primarily lecture-based and no software needs to be pre-installed.
However, to participate a bit more actively, the following tools can optionally be installed, ideally on a separate screen:
  - Latest QGIS LTR version for Windows, macOS, or Linux[ https://download.qgis.org](<https://download.qgis.org/>)
  - VS Code text editor
