Tired of start/stop editing for every single layer in your project with mixed data sources?Starting from version 3.26, QGIS has a new transaction mode called « Buffered Transaction Groups ». Within this mode, all layers which are not read-only are put in one « transaction group » and handled together when the actions « Toggle Editing » or « Save Layer Edits » are activated. It doesn’t matter if the layers come from different providers like GeoPackage, PostgreSQL or Shapefile.
