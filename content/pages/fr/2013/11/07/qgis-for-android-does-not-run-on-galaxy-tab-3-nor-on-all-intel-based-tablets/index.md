**Update 2020:** QField, the successor of QGIS for android supports ARM v7, ARM 64 bit, x86 and x86_64.  
Hi all, just a quick note to remind you all that currently QGIS for android runs on ARM v7 tablets only. I currently have no time to make an x86 build and since I got two emails asking why the galaxy 3 didn’t run qgis I wanted to put out a quick warning. And remember to contribute to the tested device list [here](<https://hub.qgis.org/projects/android-qgis/wiki/Tested_Devices>)
