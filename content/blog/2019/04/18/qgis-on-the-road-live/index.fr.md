We are extremely pleased to announce the QGIS on the road tour with three **free events** this spring all over Switzerland. Limited places available so act fast reserve your place for the location you want.
  - [**Bern 24.09.19**](<https://ti.to/opengisch/qgis-on-the-road-bern>)**13:40 (event held in German)**
  - [**Lucerne 22.10.19**](<https://ti.to/opengisch/qgis-on-the-road-luzern>)**13:40 (event held in German)**
  - [Bellinzona 14.05.19](<https://ti.to/opengisch/qgis-on-the-road-bellinzona>) 14:10 (event held in Italian)
  - [Zürich 21.05.19](<https://ti.to/opengisch/qgis-on-the-road-zurich>) 13:40 (event held in German)
  - [Lausanne 28.05.19](<https://ti.to/opengisch/qgis-on-the-road-lausanne>) 13:40 (event held in French)


Telling the story of Ms Maya Mielina, a retired GIS analyst and passionate beekeeper, our [QGIS experts](</about/index.html>) will show you features that you might not even have imagined existed in QGIS and that will allow you to dramatically increase your efficiency.
The format of the event is not that of a classic workshop but rather a prolonged presentation of extremely useful features. To keep the presentation interesting we will not focus on details or give a step by step tutorial, instead, will give you the gist of the idea thanks to our videos.
We advise you to take notes and to rewatch the published videos after the event.
