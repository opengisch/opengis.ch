Yesterday I had the possibility to present QGIS on Android at the [QGIS Swiss user meeting 2012](<https://www.qgis.org/en/user-meetings/bern2012.html>) held at the Bern University. In the morning I held [this presentation](</wp-content/uploads/2012/06/qgis-for-android-short.odp>) and in the afternoon I leaded a small [workshop](</wp-content/uploads/2012/06/labSession.odp>) where we discussed more in depth how qgis of android works. I’d like to thank the QGIS user group Switzerland for organizing and the sponsors for supporting such a nice event.
