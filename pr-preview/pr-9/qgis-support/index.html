QGIS Support & maintenance

“We deeply care about you and your deployments and this is why we take support and maintenance very seriously and are proud to offer support contracts with SLAs. Furthermore, by choosing one of our support contracts you help our QGIS sustainability initiative.”

Marco Bernasocchi, CEO

Choose Your Plan

An OPENGIS.ch support contract guarantees you a team of geospatial experts at your fingertips. Thanks to a state of the art support platform and dedicated SLA s you will always get your answers on time. Furthermore, by choosing an OPENGIS.ch support contract you support our QGIS sustainability initiative and thus help QGIS to grow sustainably. For every support contract of 10 days or more, we donate additional days to the QGIS sustainability initiative. Instead of being forfeited, all unused hours at the end of the 12 months of each contract are also donated to the QGIS sustainability initiative.

What is covered?

  • Current version of QGIS according to qgis.org
  • Current Long Term Release (LTR) of QGIS according to qgis.org
  • Current QField Version according to qfield.org
  • Python programming language in the context of QGIS
  • Other system components related to QGIS that are relevant to the successful operation of your geospatial infrastructure
